Tacos

**This recipe is a parody based on the song "White People Taco Night"**

Description

A fun Mexican dish bastardized by the creation of White People Taco Night (WPTN). It's not limited to only white people, but white people love it the most.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells from the grocery store
  • Ground beef from the grocery store
  • Shredded cheese from the grocery store
  • Ortega sauce from the grocery store

Steps

  1. Sit at the table
  2. Sprinkle everything into the shell
  3. Eat each taco as fast as you can
  4. Have another taco (or two, or three, or four!)
