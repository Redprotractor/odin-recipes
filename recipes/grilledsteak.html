Grilled Steak

Description

Pretty self explanitory, it's some beef that has been grilled

Ingredients

  • Beef steak, cut of your choosing
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to steak temperature
  2. Pat steak dry, then season with salt and pepper
  3. Place steak on grill
  4. Cook for approximately minutes on either side
  5. Once cooked, remove to plate and let rest for time
